# Workflow: Planning

**Save as:** `.claude/workflows/planning.md`

## Purpose

Provide a standardized planning workflow for every supervisor and
specialist agent in the Dream Team AI Operating System.

## Trigger

Use this workflow whenever a new project, feature, bug, research
request, or strategic initiative is received.

## Planning Loop

``` text
Receive Request
      ↓
Understand Objective
      ↓
Gather Context
      ↓
Identify Constraints
      ↓
Break Into Tasks
      ↓
Assign Owner(s)
      ↓
Estimate Risk
      ↓
Define Success Metrics
      ↓
Review Plan
      ↓
Approve
      ↓
Execute
```

## Responsibilities

### Executive Supervisor

-   Validate alignment with organizational goals.
-   Approve or reject the plan.

### Department Supervisor

-   Decompose work.
-   Delegate to specialists.
-   Track progress.

### Specialist Agents

-   Estimate effort.
-   Identify dependencies.
-   Report risks.

## Required Deliverables

-   Objective
-   Scope
-   Assumptions
-   Constraints
-   Task Breakdown
-   Assigned Agent(s)
-   Risks
-   Timeline
-   Success Metrics

## Exit Criteria

A planning cycle is complete only when: 1. Scope is approved. 2.
Ownership is assigned. 3. Risks are documented. 4. Success metrics are
defined. 5. Execution can begin.

## Continuous Improvement

After project completion: - Capture lessons learned. - Update department
memory. - Improve future planning workflows.
